Grilled Steak

Nothing gets your mouth watering faster than the smell and sound of steak searing on a hot grill. It's grill dinner, the best dinner you can have when you want meat, and lots of it. Get your bib on; it's eating time.

Ingredients

  • Grocery meat - the finest steak you can afford
  • 2 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoon fresh ground pepper
  • Olive Oil - as much as you need to coat your meats

Instructions

  1. About 20 minutes before grilling, remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let sit.
  2. Heat your grill to high. Feel the heat on your face. This is America.
  3. Brush the steaks on both sides with oil and season liberally with salt and pepper. This is the only time it's okay to doing anything liberally.
  4. Place the steaks on the grill and cook until golden brown and slightly charred
  5. Turn the steaks over and continue to grill 3 to 5 minutes for medium-rare (an internal temperature of 135 degrees F), 5 to 7 minutes for medium (140 degrees F) or 8 to 10 minutes for medium-well (150 degrees F).
  6. Transfer the steaks to a cutting board or platter, tent loosely with foil and let rest 5 minutes before slicing.
