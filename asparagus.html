Grilled Asparagus

When you're hosting a grill dinner, you can't slap a steak on the grill and call it a day. You need side dishes, or your guests will eat too much meat and sweat all over your newly upholstered rattan chairs. No one wants that!

You want to know what I serve my guests hungry guests whenever it's grill dinner time? Asparagus! It's healthy, delicious, and the rank odor of their urine will remind them of my fantastic cooking for days.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound asparagus (chunky bois are best)
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • lemon wedges , if desired

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat, about 350° to 400°F
  2. Remove the woody ends of the asparagus (unless ya nasty)
  3. Place the asparagus spears onto a foil-lined baking sheet
  4. Drizzle in olive oil and toss to coat
  5. Season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper and toss again
  6. Place the asparagus across the grill grates perpendicular to the bars
  7. Grill with the lid closed for 6 to 10 minutes or until tender and crisp, rolling with tongs for even grill marks
  8. Finish with a squeeze of lemon
